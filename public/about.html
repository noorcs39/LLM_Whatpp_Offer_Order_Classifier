Our Story

Founded in 2024, BagOffer emerged from a simple observation: the bag industry needed a smarter way to connect buyers and sellers. Traditional methods were slow, inefficient, and missed opportunities for both customers looking for specific bags and suppliers with excess inventory.

Our platform leverages the power of WhatsApp's global reach combined with cutting-edge AI technology to create seamless connections. By automatically classifying messages, translating communications, and matching compatible offers and orders, we've transformed how the bag industry operates.

Today, we serve thousands of customers and suppliers across multiple countries, processing hundreds of messages daily and facilitating successful business relationships that drive the bag industry forward.
